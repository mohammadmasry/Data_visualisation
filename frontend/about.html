About the Project

The ECG Real-Time Monitoring System is designed to track heart rates and provide real-time data visualization for healthcare purposes. By offering a simple and effective monitoring solution, the system enables individuals and healthcare providers to observe and analyze heart health efficiently.

How It Works

The system uses advanced sensors to measure heartbeats and processes the data through microcontrollers. The collected data is displayed in real time, providing detailed insights into the user's heart activity, which can be used for further analysis or medical assessments.

Key Features

  • Accurate heart rate monitoring with real-time data visualization.
  • User-friendly interface for ease of use.
  • Data storage for long-term trend analysis.
  • Scalable design for integration with additional systems or devices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a reliable and accessible tool for continuous heart rate monitoring. We strive to enhance healthcare experiences by providing tools that allow users to better understand their heart health and collaborate effectively with medical professionals.
